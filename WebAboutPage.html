Ready To Get Out Affordable Housing and Property in Kenya today?

As the world's population expands, finding suitable accommodation is a global problem.
The global housing gap is estimated at 330 million households, and that is just in urban environments.
By 2025, this number is expected to grow by 30 per cent, leaving 1.6 billion people without secure, affordable housing.
In Kenya, affordable housing is part of the Big 4 Agenda and among key points in vision 2030.
Technology is a very important tool to improve and attain the goals set for this country and that is why we decided to create this Website.
